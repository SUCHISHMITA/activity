Basic Mathematical Operations for Tinu

We are using two variables:

Variable 1 (num1): 20
Variable 2 (num2): 5

Results:

Addition (+)

20 + 5 = 25

Subtraction (-)

20 - 5 = 15

Multiplication (*)

20 * 5 = 100

Division (/)

20 / 5 = 4

Modulus (%) - Remainder

Remainder of 20 divided by 5 (20 % 5) = 0
